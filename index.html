About

Dorothy Akoth comes from a nuclear family she's the fifth born in a family of six, some of her hobbies are Interacting with Kids, swimming and singing. She went to Xaverian Secondary School in Kisumu County and Currently she is a Student at AkiraChix Campus in Nairobi County pursuing Computer Science. Through the skills she has in Computer Science,her biggest dream is to set up a free space in Siaya County where young adolescent girls are able to come and interact freely with their peers at the same time Studying computer Science hence achieving their dreams. She is a Tech Enthuasist, Community Activist She believes that Women can build, lead and win with no limitations

Projects

In her volunteering work with Wise-kenya Organization under Leadership Development Department , she was able to come up with a dustbin project, with the team of young adolescent girls in Kisumu County around the Lake Region in Dunga Community.

Her team was able to set up three dustbins to people living in Dunga Community, this was to help them in environmental pollution in that keeping the fishing areas clean.

During the Covid-19 pandemic last year , together with her team , that is team Wise-Girls, they visited different households who had elderly women around Dunga Community.We provided them with food, washed dishes for them and even cooked for them.

This was the best volunteering Experience she have ever had

I had the opportunity to participate at the Youth in Blue Economy in Kisumu organized by the Youth Congress through support by the Canadian High Commission.

Humour and how Impacted Trevor Noah's Life
